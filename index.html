Representation Theory on Ice
Part I: Linköping University — 31 Jan–1 Feb 2026
Part II: Vetenskapens hus, Luleå — 3–4 Feb 2026
Optional: Jokkmokk Winter Market — 5 Feb 2026

Overview

Representation Theory on Ice is a two-part winter workshop in Sweden 2026: Linköping (Part I) followed by Luleå (Part II). The meeting focuses on representation theory and related areas, with time for discussion and collaboration.

Two locations — attend one or both.
Travel between parts is planned on 2 February (morning). Program details will appear on the Program page.
